In the heart of San Francisco—the birthplace of CTOS 2.0 and Silicon Valley’s digital empire—the Council stands as DedSec’s core resistance cell. Composed of elite hackers, engineers, and rogue visionaries, the Council operates in the shadows to dismantle surveillance, exploit corrupt systems, and ignite the spark of digital rebellion. They are not just coders—they are culture-jammers, truth-smugglers, and freedom architects. Every line of code, every leak, every breach is a strike against control. This is where the revolution begins.

We are not thieves. We are not murderers. We are awake.

MARCUS HOLLOWAY

The brilliant hacker and main character. Framed by CTOS 2.0 for a crime he didn't commit, Marcus joins DedSec to expose the corruption of corporations and reclaim freedom for the people. He's smart, witty, and deeply believes in using technology for liberation.
