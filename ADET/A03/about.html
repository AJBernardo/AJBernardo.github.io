THE ONES BEHIND THE MASK

DedSec is an underground network of hackers united by a single mission: to expose corruption and fight for freedom in a world controlled by surveillance and greed. We believe in transparency, truth, and the unstoppable power of the people. We are not criminals. We are the resistance. We are DedSec.

OUR HEADQUARTERS

We don’t do offices. We build battlegrounds. Every DedSec HQ is a living, breathing weapon — born from scrap, street art, and raw defiance. Hidden in Chicago’s shadows, burning bright in San Francisco’s heart, and rising from London’s underground, our hideouts are where revolutions ignite. These are the places where the corrupt are exposed, where systems crash, and where freedom is hard-coded into existence. No suits, no rules — just rebels, machines, and the will to tear it all down. You want in? Find us. We are everywhere. We are DedSec.
