The Assassin's Creed franchise is a globally renowned series of video games, novels, comics, and multimedia adaptations created by Ubisoft. It debuted in 2007 with a game set during the Third Crusade and has since evolved into a sprawling franchise known for its unique blend of historical exploration and science fiction.

ASSASSIN'S CREED SHADOWS2025

Will be Release on February 14, 2025

Assassin's Creed Shadows is an upcoming entry in the Assassin's Creed series, set for release in early 2025. The game takes place during Feudal Japan's Azuchi-Momoyama era, featuring two protagonists: Yasuke, the first Black samurai, and Naoe, a skilled shinobi. Yasuke’s combat is heavy and brutal, while Naoe focuses on stealth and agility. Players can switch between them to suit their preferred playstyle. The game promises a detailed open world, with historical accuracy and dynamic combat.​
