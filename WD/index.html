WD PROJECTS

Welcome to my collection of web development course projects! These projects showcase my journey in learning and applying front-end and back-end technologies. Each project reflects my growth in coding, problem-solving, and UI/UX design. From interactive websites to dynamic web applications, explore how I’ve transformed ideas into functional digital experiences.

01 SAMSUNG GALAXY Z FLIP 5

This project highlights the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 5 through a modern and visually appealing website. It showcases the phone’s unique foldable design, key features, and specifications in an engaging and informative way. The project focuses on delivering a user-friendly interface to enhance the product presentation.

02 BELLA VENEZIA

This project captures the timeless charm of Venice, Italy, showcasing its iconic landmarks like St. Mark’s Basilica, the Grand Canal, and the Rialto Bridge. Explore hidden alleys, breathtaking architecture, and the city’s enchanting atmosphere through a beautifully designed web experience.

03 IDOL FACTORY

This project highlights the talented actors of IdolFactory Thailand, featuring detailed profiles, career highlights, and their most popular dramas. Explore in-depth information on their roles, upcoming projects, and behind-the-scenes moments from hit series.

04 THE HOGWARTS LEDGER

This API powers Hogwarts Ledger, providing detailed profiles of Hogwarts characters, including house, wand, patronus, and background. Designed for easy integration, it’s a go-to resource for fans and developers exploring the Wizarding World.

05 CHESS CITADEL

This project is a chess resource platform focused on openings, game mechanics, and the latest chess news. Explore detailed analyses of classic and modern openings, stay updated on tournaments, and deepen your understanding of chess strategies through expert insights.

06 ASSASSIN'S CREED PARALLAX

This project is an interactive parallax website that features the entire Assassin’s Creed game franchise. Explore the evolution of the series with stunning visuals, game summaries, and key highlights from each title. With smooth scrolling effects and immersive design, this project brings the Assassin’s Creed universe to life like never before.
